Salmon

Picture of salmon cooked in the air fryer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and papper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Lemon wedges
  • 4 salmon fillets

Steps:

  1. Cut the salmon fillets into even sizes and pre heat your air fryer to 400Â°F.
  2. Rub each fillet with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper. Place the salmon in the air fryer and air fry for 7-9 minutes, depending on this thickness of the salmon. Please note, time may vary between air fryers.
  3. Open basket and check for desired doneness with a fork.
